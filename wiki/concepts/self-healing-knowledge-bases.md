# Self-Healing Knowledge Bases

> Self-healing knowledge bases automatically detect and repair inconsistencies, missing links, or stale information through linting, validation, and maintenance loops. [Karpathy on LLM knowledge bases](../../raw/tweets/karpathy-llm-knowledge-bases-something-i-m-finding-very-us.md)

## Why It Matters

This is the bridge between a static wiki and a living system: every query or lint pass becomes a chance to improve the corpus itself. [OpenViking source](../../raw/repos/volcengine-openviking.md)
