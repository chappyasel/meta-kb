# Multi-Hop Retrieval

> Multi-hop retrieval answers complex questions by chaining or aggregating multiple retrieval steps instead of relying on one nearest-neighbor lookup. [HippoRAG source](../../raw/repos/osu-nlp-group-hipporag.md)

## Why It Matters

It is one of the main reasons graph-based and hierarchy-aware retrieval systems continue to outperform flat lookup on harder reasoning tasks. [RAG vs. GraphRAG](../../raw/papers/han-rag-vs-graphrag-a-systematic-evaluation-and-key.md)
