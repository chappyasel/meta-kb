# Knowledge Graphs

> Knowledge graphs represent entities and their relationships as structured graphs, supporting retrieval, reasoning, and memory organization. [Zep paper](../../raw/papers/rasmussen-zep-a-temporal-knowledge-graph-architecture-for-a.md)

## Why It Matters

They provide the structural backbone for systems that need more than text similarity, especially when provenance and multi-hop reasoning matter. [Graphiti source](../../raw/repos/getzep-graphiti.md)
