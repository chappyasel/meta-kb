# User Profiling

> User profiling is the process of building and updating structured representations of user preferences, history, and characteristics to personalize agent behavior. [Mem0 source](../../raw/repos/mem0ai-mem0.md)

## Why It Matters

It is one of the most common production uses of persistent memory, and it is why many memory systems emphasize stable facts plus recent activity instead of only raw transcripts. [Supermemory source](../../raw/repos/supermemoryai-supermemory.md)
