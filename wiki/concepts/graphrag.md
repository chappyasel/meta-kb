# GraphRAG

> GraphRAG uses graph structure to organize retrieval, enabling relationship-aware and often multi-hop grounding for generation. [RAG vs. GraphRAG](../../raw/papers/han-rag-vs-graphrag-a-systematic-evaluation-and-key.md)

## Why It Matters

GraphRAG matters because it often outperforms flat retrieval on complex relational tasks, even if simple lookup still favors lighter approaches. [HippoRAG source](../../raw/repos/osu-nlp-group-hipporag.md) [RAG vs. GraphRAG](../../raw/papers/han-rag-vs-graphrag-a-systematic-evaluation-and-key.md)
