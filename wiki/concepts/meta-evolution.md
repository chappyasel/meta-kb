# Meta-Evolution

> Meta-evolution is the process by which a system improves not only its behavior, but also the mechanisms it uses to improve itself. [Darwin Godel Machine paper](../../raw/papers/zhang-darwin-godel-machine-open-ended-evolution-of-self.md)

## Why It Matters

It is the longer-horizon ambition behind open-ended self-improving systems, where the loop itself becomes mutable and subject to optimization. [Darwin Godel Machine paper](../../raw/papers/zhang-darwin-godel-machine-open-ended-evolution-of-self.md)
