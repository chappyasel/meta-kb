# Context Bloat

> Context bloat is the operational problem where too much always-on context makes an agent slower, noisier, and less accurate. [Anthropic on context engineering](../../raw/articles/effective-context-engineering-for-ai-agents.md)

## Why It Matters

It is the practical failure mode that leads teams toward skills, compaction, tiered loading, and smaller control planes. [Martin Fowler on coding-agent context engineering](../../raw/articles/martinfowler-com-context-engineering-for-coding-agents.md)
