# Hallucination Compounding

> Hallucination compounding is the failure mode where small errors accumulate across multi-step agent runs and become increasingly costly or hard to unwind. [Anthropic on context engineering](../../raw/articles/effective-context-engineering-for-ai-agents.md)

## Why It Matters

It is one reason long-horizon systems now rely on traces, review gates, and durable notes instead of trusting one uninterrupted chain of reasoning. [LangChain traces](../../raw/articles/langchain-com-the-agent-improvement-loop-starts-with-a-trace.md)
