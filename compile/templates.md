# Article Templates

## Synthesis article (Tier 1, 2000-4000 words)

```markdown
# The State of [Bucket Name]

> [2-3 sentence summary of the state of play in this area]

## Approach Categories

### [Category 1 Name]
[Describe the approach. Name key projects implementing it. Compare tradeoffs.
Cite specific sources: [Source](../raw/type/file.md)]

### [Category 2 Name]
...

### [Category 3 Name]
...

## The Convergence
[What threads keep emerging? What is everyone converging on?]

## The Divergence
[What unique or contrarian approaches stand out? What's different and why?]

## What's Hot Now
[Momentum signals. Recent launches. Viral discussions. Cite star counts,
engagement numbers. Reference specific tweets and releases.]

## Where It's Going
[Trends. Emerging patterns. Open research questions.]

## Open Questions
- [Question the field hasn't answered yet]
- [Gap where more research/tools are needed]

## Sources
[List all sources used, grouped by type]
```

## Project reference card (Tier 3, 300-500 words)

```markdown
# [Project Name]

> [1-2 sentence summary + key differentiator]

## What It Does
[Concrete description. Not marketing copy.]

## Architecture
[How it's built. Key technical decisions.]

## Key Numbers
[Stars, benchmarks, adoption metrics. Be specific.]

## Strengths
- [Strength with evidence]

## Limitations
- [Honest limitation — no hedging]

## Alternatives
- [Alternative](../projects/alt.md) — use when [specific scenario]

## Sources
- [Source](../raw/type/file.md) — [key quote]
```

## Concept explainer (Tier 3, 500-1000 words)

```markdown
# [Concept Name]

> [1-2 sentence definition]

## Why It Matters
[Why practitioners should care about this concept]

## How It Works
[The mechanism, with concrete examples]

## Who Implements It
- [Project](../projects/slug.md) — how they implement this concept

## Open Questions
- [What's unresolved about this concept]

## Sources
- [Source](../raw/type/file.md) — [key quote]
```
