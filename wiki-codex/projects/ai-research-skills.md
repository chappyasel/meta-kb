# AI-Research-SKILLs

> A large research-focused skills registry that aims to cover the full research lifecycle, not just isolated framework usage. [Source](../../raw/repos/orchestra-research-ai-research-skills.md)

## What It Does

AI-Research-SKILLs packages research and engineering expertise for literature review, ideation, experiments, training, evaluation, deployment, and paper writing, with an autoresearch orchestration layer routing work to the right domain skill. [Source](../../raw/repos/orchestra-research-ai-research-skills.md)

## Architecture

The library spans 22 categories and 87 skills. Its central abstraction is not only the individual skill but also the orchestration layer that composes them into a two-loop research workflow. [Source](../../raw/repos/orchestra-research-ai-research-skills.md)

## Key Numbers

- 6,111 GitHub stars. [Source](../../raw/repos/orchestra-research-ai-research-skills.md)
- 87 skills across 22 categories. [Source](../../raw/repos/orchestra-research-ai-research-skills.md)

## Strengths

- Unusually strong lifecycle coverage from ideation to paper writing. [Source](../../raw/repos/orchestra-research-ai-research-skills.md)
- Good example of skills becoming a workflow graph, not just a library. [Source](../../raw/repos/orchestra-research-ai-research-skills.md)

## Limitations

- The project is broad enough that teams outside AI research may only need a small slice of it. [Source](../../raw/repos/orchestra-research-ai-research-skills.md)
